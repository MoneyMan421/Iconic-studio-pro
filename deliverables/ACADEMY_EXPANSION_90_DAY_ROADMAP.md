# Academy Expansion — 90-Day Roadmap

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Objective
Build a repeatable growth system for the academy by expanding offers, content, community, partnerships, retention, and acquisition while tracking unit economics.

---

## Phase 1 (Days 1–30): Foundation + Offer Setup

### 1) Offer Ladder
- Finalize 3-tier path:
  - Free Beginner Track
  - Paid Pro Track
  - Certification Track
- Package upsells:
  - Done-for-you templates
  - Project file bundle

### 2) Content Engine Setup
- Define curriculum map:
  - Beginner
  - Intermediate
  - Pro
- Create publishing rhythm:
  - 3 short lessons per week
  - 1 deep tutorial per week
- Build a 4-week content backlog before launch.

### 3) Retention Infrastructure
- Implement onboarding checklist.
- Add progress tracking by module completion.
- Set email flows:
  - Welcome
  - Inactive nudge
  - Milestone celebration

### 4) Measurement Baseline
- Establish dashboard metrics:
  - CAC
  - Activation rate
  - Course completion rate
  - Churn
  - LTV
  - Referral rate
- Capture baseline numbers before scaling.

---

## Phase 2 (Days 31–60): Launch + Community + Partnerships

### 1) Community Flywheel
- Launch private student community (Discord or Telegram).
- Start monthly design challenge.
- Publish leaderboard and weekly student spotlight.

### 2) Monetization Expansion
- Launch pricing options:
  - Subscription plan
  - Lifetime bundle
- Scope team/agency and institutional plans.

### 3) Partnerships
- Run first creator collaboration wave.
- Outreach to design schools and app communities.
- Launch affiliate/referral program with tracking links and payout rules.

### 4) Acquisition Foundation
- Publish SEO-focused tutorials weekly.
- Build lead magnet funnel (free template pack + email capture).
- Add YouTube CTA funnel into the free track.

---

## Phase 3 (Days 61–90): Optimization + Scale

### 1) Funnel Optimization
- Audit free-to-paid conversion path.
- Improve weak steps in:
  - Landing page
  - Onboarding
  - Checkout
  - Activation sequence

### 2) Retention Optimization
- Add milestone badges and completion certificates.
- Expand reminder sequences by learner segment.
- Add reactivation campaign for churn-risk users.

### 3) Growth Scale Rules
- Scale only channels meeting target LTV/CAC threshold.
- Keep paid ads capped until organic funnel conversion remains stable for 2+ consecutive review cycles.

### 4) Offer Expansion Decision
- Decide whether to launch:
  - Cohort-based bootcamp
  - Team/agency plan
  - Certification v2
- Use KPI evidence from prior 60 days.

---

## KPI Targets (Initial)

- Activation rate: +20% from baseline
- Completion rate: +15% from baseline
- Referral rate: +10% from baseline
- Churn: -15% from baseline
- LTV/CAC ratio: maintain at or above 3.0 before ad scale

---

## Weekly Operating Cadence

- Weekly:
  - Review KPI dashboard
  - Review content output and funnel conversion
  - Prioritize top 3 bottlenecks
- Monthly:
  - Offer performance review
  - Community engagement review
  - Partnership ROI review

---

## Definition of Success at Day 90

- Working offer ladder with active upsells
- Consistent content publishing cadence
- Active community challenge cycle and referrals
- At least one reliable acquisition channel with healthy LTV/CAC
- Documented decision on next expansion lever (bootcamp, team plans, or certification expansion)
